What is Wagmi Labs Inc?

Wagmi Labs Inc (“Wagmi”) is a C-Corp set up by three Ohmies to help family offices and institutions invest in the Olympus ecosystem. Wagmi will use majority of its revenues to hire developers, designers and engineers to accelerate growth of Olympus ecosystem.

Wagmi is a C-Corp in name but will be run like a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO). DAO contributors wanting to work in crypto full time can join Wagmi to avail benefits typically associated with full time employment without compromising their autonomy.

About

Wagmi will offer two products to investors:

3.3% fee for passive buy and stake

9.9% fee for a leveraged long strategy

We will not time investments or manage money for our customers. We will act as an interface that can accept payments in fiat and provide relevant insights about the Olympus ecosystem.
